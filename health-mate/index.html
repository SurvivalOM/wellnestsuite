Personal Health Mate 💚 Coming soon

Prevention beats cure. This is your companion for it.

Most health apps count steps or scare you with symptoms. Personal Health Mate is different: it is a lifelong companion that helps you understand your own body — system by system — build kind daily routines, and walk into every doctor's appointment with better questions. It never diagnoses, never prescribes, and never replaces your doctor.

What it will do at launch

  • A body-systems dashboard — a clear, friendly picture of the 12 systems of your body, with transparent readings based only on what you tell it ("based on what you told us — not a medical assessment").
  • Honest root-cause education — plain-language explanations of how lifestyle, sleep, stress and movement shape common issues, with sources.
  • Daily routines and gentle reminders — your doctor says "walk 40 minutes"? It becomes a scheduled, celebrated habit.
  • Safety first, always — emergency signs are screened before anything else, and serious matters are warmly redirected to professionals. Safety features will never sit behind a paywall.
  • Your data, guarded — health data is encrypted, never sold, never shared with the other WellNest apps without your explicit say-so, and deletable in one click.
