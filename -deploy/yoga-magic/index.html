Fair questions

Is it safe if I have a health condition?

Yoga Magic applies commonly taught modifications for 12 conditions automatically — and still, please clear any new exercise practice with your doctor first. The app will remind you of the same.

Do I need experience?

No. Sessions begin gently and progress only as you do.

Where is my data?

On your device. The app is offline-first and has no account in v1.

A caring reminder: Yoga Magic offers commonly taught practice guidance, not medical advice. Please consult a qualified doctor before beginning any new practice, especially with an existing condition.
