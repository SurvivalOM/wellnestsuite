Fair questions

Is this medical advice?

No. Ayurveda Master teaches the traditional system of Ayurveda as it is described in its classical texts — it is education, not diagnosis or treatment. For any health condition, please see a qualified doctor.

From public-domain classical sources (Charaka and Sushruta in verified translations), always cited, never invented.

No. The app runs on your device and works offline; your answers never leave it.
